Hacker Space

Hackerspace.SG is Singapore's very own Hackerspace. Built by and for geeks, nerds, inventors, engineers, and entrepreneurs, Hackerspace.SG is the Singapore hacker community's home, living room and laboratory.

Come hang out with the community in the evenings. Co-work with us during the day. We also host regular events, meetups, presentations, workshops, and movie nights for the local community.

9am - 6pm
71 Ayer Rajah Crescent
#05-16, Singapore 139951
Free

The Hub Singapore

Building ecosystems that bridge early stage startups to mature ventures, The Hub Singapore is Singapore’s largest community of entrepreneurs, creatives and techies. We are a coworking space in the heart of Singapore and also offer mentorship, incubation, workshops, events, marketing support, shared services, regional outreach and a dedicated corporate innovation program.

Please pre-register at bit.ly/hubpass before coming.

9am - 6pm
128 Prinsep Street, Singapore 188655
Free

The Co

The Co. is an award-winning ecosystem of workspaces, and is home to a community of global businesses and digital companies.

Registration with The Co personnel is required at the entrance. Please bring Devfest identification, and call in advance to ensure availability on the day of booking.

9am - 6pm
75 High St, Singapore 179435

Woolf Works

Woolf Works is a shared work space for women, located on lively Joo Chiat Rd, on the east coast of Singapore. We are a calm, quiet oasis in which you can really be productive. We also have an awesome coffee machine and a great community of women to chat to over your lunch break.

9am - 6pm
176 Joo Chiat Rd, Singpaore 427447

Workhouse

Workhouse is a coworking space located in the heart of Little India. Designed with startups and small businesses in mind, Workhouse aims to be a platform that fosters creative collaborations between community members by promoting entrepreneurship, diversity, and work with a social directive.

Just like our Facebook page and come down between 9 - 6 pm to use our space! (Subject to Availability, please call to reserve before coming down)

9am - 6pm
2 Veerasamy Rd, Singapore 207305
Free from 17th - 23rd November

Ace Ideation Center
